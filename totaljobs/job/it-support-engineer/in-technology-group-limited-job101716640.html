Job Title: IT Support Engineer
Location: Colchester
Salary: £25,000 - £35,000

Are you a skilled IT professional with a passion for troubleshooting and providing top-notch technical support? My client are seeking an IT Support Engineer to join their forward-thinking team in the Colchester area. As an IT Support Engineer, you will play a crucial role in maintaining and enhancing their IT infrastructure, ensuring seamless operations for the organization.

Key Responsibilities:
* Provide 1st & 2nd line technical support to end-users, resolving hardware and software issues promptly.
* Troubleshoot and diagnose IT problems, implementing effective solutions.
* Install, configure, and maintain computer systems and applications.
* Collaborate with the IT team to implement and enhance IT policies and procedures.
* Assist in the management and maintenance of IT infrastructure.

Qualifications and Skills:
* Proven experience in IT support roles.
* Windows Server Experience (2016/2019/2022)
* Virtualisation Knowledge
* MDM - Intune/Jamf Pro
* Familiarity with network protocols and troubleshooting.
* Excellent problem-solving and communication skills.
* Customer-oriented with a proactive approach to issue resolution.

Education and Certifications:
* A degree in IT, Computer Science, or a related field is preferred.
* MSP Experience is desireable
* Relevant certifications such as CompTIA A+, Microsoft Certified: Windows 10, etc., are advantageous.

Interviews are being arranged immediately around YOUR schedule so hit apply now to be immediately considered.

In Technology Group Ltd is acting as an Employment Agency in relation to this vacancy.
